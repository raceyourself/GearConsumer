Why heart rate

Tracking your heart rate tells you exactly how hard, or how easy, you're running. Heart-rate training is a great way to improve your health and fitness efficiently. Measuring your heart rate can help you keep from making one of the most common mistakes that runners of all abilities make; running too fast too often, which puts you at risk for injury and burnout. Have fun, stay safe and always consult a doctor before undertaking a new exercise regimen.
